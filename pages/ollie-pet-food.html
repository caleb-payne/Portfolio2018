Digital marketing materials for a subscription dog food startup

My Ollie

Fall 2017When you love your pup, you want the best for them. Ollie is a high-quality, human grade dog food that gets delivered to your door on a subscription basis.

Most of their customer base is reached through digital channels, especially social media. I had the opportunity to work with them in-house on several marketing pieces of various scope. This is a collection of some of those.

Woof

Prompts and scripts were typically provided for me with the exception of a few smaller pieces. However, given the scrappy startup environment, even my pre-ideated projects were largely self-directed once they began.

This allowed me to figure out my out best practices and learn how to work toward a company's marketing goals without explicitly being told what to do every step of the way.
