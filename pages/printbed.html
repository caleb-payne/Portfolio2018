NMD Interactive III

Printbed

Image sharing app for the maker community

  • DECK.pdf↗

It's an image sharing platform for people with a passion for 3D printing and other kinds of making.

Overview

Spring 2016Doing-it-yourself brings a special kind of satisfaction: It's pride from problem solving. It's a rush of self-sufficience and the respectful envy of your peers. It's the fuzzy warmth of a tightly woven community.

PrintBed elevates this philosophy by taking advantage of current technology. It's an image sharing platform for people with a passion for 3D printing and other kinds of self-directed making. PrintBed makes it easy for high school hobbyists, innovative artists, pragmatic engineers and cool dads alike to share their stories and get inspired.

Conclusion

PrintBed is an example of image sharing for a niche market. Image apps have been done before, but this was an opportunity for me to pay special attention to narrower base. It was also a good canvas for trying out microinteractions. My next steps would be to include a more robust information architecture and finer-tuned mechanisms for social interaction, such as commenting and sharing.

This speculative project was completed as part of the New Media Design Interactive III course curriculum at Rochester Institute of Technology.
