Mercyhurst Preparatory School

Anything & Everything

High school admissions recruiting video

Context

Fall 2016When attracting applicants, private high schools invite 7th and 8th graders to shadow their students one-on-one for a day. This brings multitudes of nervous elementary school kids on campus to get a feel for the place and more comfortably address what might feel like the most important decision of their lives.

Following the success of a previous project we had done together, Mercyhurst Preparatory School asked me to create an updated video that they could show to prospective students on shadow day.

Approach

I worked with the administration at Mercyhurst Prep to figure out a strategy that met their enrollment needs while allowing me to stretch my skills a little bit. Since it had been a few years since I attended MPS, we asked the english department to gather original works of poetry from the students themselves on the theme of being a Mercyhurst Prep Laker.

Leveraging these submissions, I wrote a script that embraced the challenges of high school and called out the benefits of a Mercyhurst Prep education. The story is told from a student’s perspective – a junior named Kelsey who is in the midst of writing a biology assignment.

Shout Out

At the time of taking this job, I was extremely influenced by Metamorphosis, the award-winning homage that Buck created for Good Books. This film allows the commercial aspect to hitchhike in the backseat of a visceral narrative. That's what I aimed for – the magic of capturing an audience with a story.

Outcome

Anything & Everything is an animated video that subverts typical marketing strategies of secondary education, welcoming students into the next chapter of their lives at MPS.

Weaknesses: needs more character animation and higher level of detail.

Strengths: some cool moments, interesting story, and a novel marketing tactic for this space. What do you think?
